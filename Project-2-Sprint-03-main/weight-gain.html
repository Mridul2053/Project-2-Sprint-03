Weight Gain

Day 1 – Chest & Triceps

  • Bench Press – 4 x 8–10 reps
  • Triceps Dips – 3 x 10 reps
  • Chest Fly – 3 x 12 reps

Day 2 – Back & Biceps

  • Seated Row – 3 x 10 reps
  • Lat Pulldown – 4 x 10 reps
  • Biceps Curl – 3 x 12 reps

Day 3 – Legs

  • Squats – 4 x 8 reps
  • Leg Press – 3 x 10 reps
  • Leg Curl – 3 x 12 reps

Day 4 – Shoulders

  • Shoulder Press – 4 x 8 reps
  • Lateral Raises – 3 x 12 reps
  • Front Raises – 3 x 12 reps

Day 5 – Full Body

  • Deadlift – 4 x 6–8 reps
  • Push-ups – 3 x 15 reps
  • Pull-ups – 3 x 8 reps
  • Plank – 3 x 30–60 sec
